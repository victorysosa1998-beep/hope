Health

A Healthy Start in Life

We believe no child should die from a preventable cause. Hope for Every Child delivers vaccinations, maternal health support, nutrition programs, and emergency medical care — even in the most dangerous places on earth.

In 2024, our health teams treated over 5 million children, delivered 2.3 million vaccine doses, and operated 340 mobile health clinics in conflict zones and remote communities.

Education

Quality Education for Every Child

Learning transforms lives. We work to close the global learning gap — ensuring children have access to safe schools, qualified teachers, and quality materials no matter where they are born or what crisis they face.

In emergencies, we set up Temporary Learning Spaces within 72 hours of a disaster, so education never stops. We have returned over 4.5 million children to school after crises since 2015.

Resilience

Building Stronger Families

We work to ensure children and their families are protected by strong social safety nets — so they can cope with shocks, break the cycle of poverty, and build futures that last beyond any single crisis.

Our cash transfer programs give families the agency to meet their children's needs directly. Our community savings groups have created over $18M in community-held assets in Africa alone.

Protection

Protecting Every Child

No child should grow up in fear. We work with partners worldwide to protect children — especially girls and those in conflict — from violence, exploitation, and abuse, both online and offline.

Our safe spaces provide emergency shelter, legal aid, and trauma-informed care. Our advocacy work has helped pass child protection legislation in 20+ countries since 2010.
