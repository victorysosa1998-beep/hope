FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my crypto donation tax-deductible?
Yes. Hope for Every Child is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Crypto donations are treated as property donations at fair market value on the date of receipt. Email us your transaction hash for an official receipt within 5 business days. Please consult a tax advisor for personal guidance.
How do I get a receipt?
After sending, email hopeforeverychild1989@yahoo.com with your transaction hash and donation amount. Our team verifies the transaction on-chain and issues a formal tax receipt within 5 business days.
What happens to the crypto after I donate?
We promptly convert crypto to USD (or local currency) to minimize market risk and speed up program delivery. All conversions and expenditures are disclosed in our annual financial reports, which are publicly available.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes. Blockchain transactions are pseudonymous — you are not required to share your identity to donate. If you wish to receive a tax receipt, you will need to contact us with your transaction details. Your personal information is never sold or shared.
Is there a minimum donation?
We welcome donations of any size. We suggest a minimum equivalent to $5 USD to ensure your donation exceeds typical network transaction fees. For very small amounts, we recommend stablecoins (USDT, USDC) on the Polygon network for the lowest fees.
Are the wallet addresses verified and secure?
Yes. All wallet addresses listed are official Hope for Every Child organizational wallets, audited annually by our financial partners. Always verify addresses on our official website at hopeforeverychild.org before sending. We will never ask for your private key or seed phrase.
