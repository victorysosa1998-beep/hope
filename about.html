Our Story

About Hope for Every Child

For over 35 years, we've fought for children who cannot fight for themselves — in classrooms, clinics, conflict zones, and the halls of power.

Our Mission

Driven by One Belief: Every Child Matters

Hope for Every Child is a global humanitarian organization dedicated to improving the lives of the world's most vulnerable children. Founded in 1989, we work in 46 countries to ensure every child has the right to survive, learn, be protected, and have a voice.

We hold governments and institutions accountable. We work with communities — not for them — because lasting change must come from within.

We believe modern giving tools — including cryptocurrency — are critical to reaching children faster, with more transparency than ever before.
